About us

Who We Are — Kenya’s Tech Community Hub

We identified a gap in Kenya’s Silicon Savannah tech ecosystem where developers, students, startups, and tech enthusiasts struggle to find tech events, free courses, internships, and developer networking in one place.

Tech Group Kenya bridges that gap from Nairobi to all 47 counties—through tech events, WhatsApp communities, Skill Me learning, our tech blog, hackathons, and career opportunities for Kenya’s growing tech ecosystem.

Got questions?

Frequently asked about Kenya’s tech community

What is Tech Group Kenya?
Tech Group Kenya is Kenya’s tech community platform and startup hub. We connect developers, students, and startups through learning resources, tech events in Nairobi and nationwide, free Skill Me courses, a tech blog, hackathons, internships, tech jobs, and networking across all 47 counties.
How do I join the Kenya tech community?
Join free via redirect.techgroupkenya.co.ke—access WhatsApp groups, social channels, event updates, and community links in one place.
What tech events do you host in Kenya?
We promote workshops, developer meetups, and conferences through Tech Events for Kenya’s tech community.
Are there free tech courses in Kenya through Skill Me?
Yes. Skill Me offers free courses, certifications, and mentorship for developers and students in Kenya.
How do I find tech jobs and internships in Kenya?
Visit TGK careers on Skill Me and join our community channels for new listings.
What services does Tech Group Kenya offer?
Tech Blog, Skill Me courses and jobs, Tech Events, WhatsApp community networking, hackathons, and career support for Kenya’s developer ecosystem.
Is Tech Group Kenya membership free?
Yes. Joining is free. Optional donations help expand tech events and outreach across Kenya’s counties.
How can I support Tech Group Kenya?
Donate via our Support page or partner with us on ecosystem collaborations.
How do I contact Tech Group Kenya in Nairobi?
Email echo@techgroupkenya.co.ke or use the contact form. We are based in Westlands, Nairobi.
Do you run hackathons and developer meetups in Kenya?
Yes—we organize and share hackathons, coding meetups, and developer networking across Kenya’s Silicon Savannah tech ecosystem.
