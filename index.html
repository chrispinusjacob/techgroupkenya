About us

Who We Are

We identified a gap in Kenya’s tech ecosystem where many developers, students, startups, and tech enthusiasts struggle to discover opportunities, connect with communities, access learning resources, and grow their careers.

Tech Group Kenya was created to bridge that gap through tech events, communities, learning platforms, blogs, networking, and career opportunities designed for Kenya’s growing tech ecosystem.

Built for Kenya’s Tech Ecosystem

Driven by community, innovation, and local expertise, we create opportunities for learning, collaboration, networking, career growth, and innovation across Kenya’s growing tech ecosystem in Kenya and the world.
