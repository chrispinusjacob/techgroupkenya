About us

Who We Are — Kenya’s Tech Community Hub

We identified a gap in Kenya’s Silicon Savannah tech ecosystem where developers, students, startups, and tech enthusiasts struggle to find tech events, free courses, internships, and developer networking in one place.

Tech Group Kenya bridges that gap from Nairobi to all 47 counties—through tech events, WhatsApp communities, Skill Me learning, our tech blog, hackathons, and career opportunities for Kenya’s growing tech ecosystem.

Got questions?

Frequently asked about Kenya’s tech community

Who is Tech Group Kenya for?
Developers, students, startups, and anyone building a career in tech in Kenya. We connect people to learning, events, jobs, and community channels nationwide.
Is it free to join?
Yes. Sign up free at redirect.techgroupkenya.co.ke for WhatsApp groups, social links, and community updates.
Where do I find hackathons and meetups in Kenya?
Browse upcoming listings on events.techgroupkenya.co.ke—workshops, conferences, and developer meetups across the country.
I’m organising an event—how do I promote it?
Submit your event on Tech Events or email echo@techgroupkenya.co.ke so we can share it with Kenya’s tech community.
Can I learn coding and tech skills for free?
Yes—Skill Me offers free courses, certifications, and mentorship.
Where do I apply for tech jobs and internships?
Apply and browse roles at skillme.techgroupkenya.co.ke/jobs. Join our channels for alerts when new openings are posted.
Can I donate or partner with TGK?
Donate at Paystack or contact us about sponsorships and collaborations.
What’s your email and office location?
Email echo@techgroupkenya.co.ke or use the contact form. We’re based in Westlands, Nairobi.
